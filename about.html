About Keven Hypnarowicz

I’m Keven Hypnarowicz. I study Information Science at Michigan State University. I like clear and simple tools that are easy to use. I enjoy learning by doing, so I am a very hands-on learner. I follow new tech because I like seeing how people are solving problems with AI.

In my free time I play games like Valorant, Fortnite, Overwatch, Apex Legends, and Marvel Rivals. I also watch Detroit sports, especially the Lions and the Tigers. Games and sports both help me reset. I try to keep my setup clean and my process simple, so I can spend more time making and less time fixing.

This blog is a place to post short updates, notes from projects, and things I am testing. I keep the writing direct and the pages light. If you like tech, games, or clean UI, you will probably find something here that fits.
